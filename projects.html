The Foundation

LeanOS

Claude skills orchestrator for building and running businesses. 19+ specialized skills handling sales, marketing, engineering, and operations autonomously. The core principle: systems beat headcount. Instead of hiring to scale, build infrastructure that lets single founders or small teams operate with the velocity of much larger organizations.

LeanOS powers both Chromly and Detekta — proving the model works across different industries (fashion tech, RegTech) with the same underlying infrastructure.

Open Source (MIT) · Claude Skills · Markdown-based
