The Problem

Founders face a scaling paradox: you need a team to grow, but you can't afford a team until you grow.

Traditional options don't work:

  • Hire engineers: $150-200k/year + equity + 3-6 months to find the right person
  • Development agencies: $50-150k per project, then they leave
  • Fractional CTO: $15-25k/month for advice, no execution
  • No-code + contractors: Hits limits fast, doesn't scale

What if you could have the capabilities of a full team without the headcount?
